The Air Conditioning Company was formed in 1986 after CEO Jeff Salmon had discovered the popularity of portable air conditioners in Asia. Mobile units had never been seen in the UK, and Jeff took a gamble on the infamous ‘British summer’ and ordered the best quality units he could find. The gamble paid off,

The gamble paid off, and the units became hugely popular all over the UK. We have continued to grow year on year, and now we have teams of air conditioning engineers who install and repair fitted systems all over Greater London and the Home Counties. Our ethos since 1986 has been (and always will be!) only to sell the highest possible quality at the keenest prices coupled with unbeatable customer care.
